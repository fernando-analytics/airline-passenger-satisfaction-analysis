# Airline Passenger Satisfaction Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes airline passenger satisfaction data to identify the factors that have the greatest impact on customer experience.

Using Python and exploratory data analysis techniques, the project investigates how service quality, flight delays, travel class, and customer loyalty influence passenger satisfaction.

The objective is to generate data-driven insights and business recommendations that could help airlines improve customer satisfaction and customer retention.

---

## Business Question

**What factors have the greatest impact on airline passenger satisfaction?**

To answer this question, the analysis focuses on:

1. Overall passenger satisfaction
2. Service quality attributes
3. Flight delays
4. Travel class
5. Customer loyalty

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains information about airline passengers, including:

- Customer demographics
- Travel characteristics
- Service ratings
- Flight delays
- Satisfaction levels

The target variable is:

- Satisfaction

which is categorized as:

- Satisfied
- Neutral or Dissatisfied

---

## Methodology

The project followed the following analytical process:

1. Data Cleaning
2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
3. Customer Segmentation
4. Service Quality Analysis
5. Delay Analysis
6. Travel Class Analysis
7. Customer Loyalty Analysis
8. Business Recommendations

---

## Key Findings

### Service Quality Is a Major Driver of Satisfaction

The largest differences between satisfied and dissatisfied passengers were observed in:

- Online Boarding
- Inflight Entertainment
- Seat Comfort
- Cleanliness

### Flight Delays Negatively Affect Satisfaction

Passengers who were neutral or dissatisfied experienced higher average departure and arrival delays.

However, service quality factors appeared to have a greater impact on satisfaction than delays alone.

### Travel Class Strongly Influences Satisfaction

Business Class passengers reported significantly higher satisfaction levels than Economy and Economy Plus passengers.

### Loyal Customers Report Higher Satisfaction

Loyal customers were substantially more satisfied than disloyal customers, suggesting a strong relationship between customer retention and customer experience.

## Business Recommendations

- Improve the digital boarding experience
- Enhance onboard comfort and entertainment
- Continue reducing operational delays
- Improve the Economy Class experience
